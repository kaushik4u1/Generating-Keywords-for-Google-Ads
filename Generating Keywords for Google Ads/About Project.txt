"Generating Keywords for Google Ads" is a project which, automatically generates keywords for a 
search engine marketing campaign using Python and Pandas library.

*Imagine working for a digital marketing agency, and the agency is approached by a massive online 
retailer of furniture. They want to test our skills at creating large campaigns for all of their 
website. We are tasked with creating a prototype set of keywords for search campaigns for their 
sofas section. The client says that they want us to generate keywords for the following products:

sofas
convertible sofas
love seats
recliners
sofa beds
